Privacy Policy for Supabase Database Explorer by Aberastegue

Effective Date: 17.09.2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Supabase Database Explorer by Aberastegue (the "Extension"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Google Chrome extension. By using the Extension, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The Supabase Database Explorer by Aberastegue Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data. The extension operates locally within your browser and does not communicate with any external servers or databases.

3. Permissions

The Extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • Active Tab: This permission allows the extension to interact with the currently active tab in your browser. It is necessary for the extension to enable the emoji search and copy functionality on the active page.

  • Storage: This permission is used to store user preferences locally on your device. This ensures that any custom settings or frequently used emojis are retained for future sessions.

  • Scripting: This permission allows the extension to inject and execute scripts within your browser tabs, which is essential for providing the emoji search and copy features.

These permissions are used solely to enhance your experience with the Supabase Database Explorer. The extension does not track your browsing activities or collect any data from your tabs or pages beyond the necessary functionality.

4. Use of Web Accessible Resources

The Extension uses web-accessible resources such as fonts to display emojis correctly across different web pages. These resources are only used to ensure the proper functionality of the extension and do not involve any data collection or tracking.

5. Data Security

Since the Extension does not collect or store any personal data, there are no specific data security measures in place. However, we are committed to maintaining the highest level of security and ensuring that the extension does not expose you to any risks.

6. Third-Party Services

The Supabase Database Explorer by Aberastegue Extension does not use or interact with any third-party services or APIs that could collect or process your data.

7. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at apps@aberastegue.com.ar.

Thank you for using Supabase Database Explorer by Aberastegue. We are committed to providing a safe and enjoyable browsing experience.
